Instructions

Welcome to gathurUP!

Step 1: Add People

Start in the People and Groups tab. Add everyone you might want to invite to your events by entering their full names. These could be friends, family, colleagues - anyone you plan activities with.

Step 2: Create Groups

Still in People and Groups, switch to the Groups section to organize your people into different groups. Each group can have its own purpose - maybe one for family gatherings, another for game nights, or weekend trips.

Step 3: Create Events

Use the Create Event tab to set up your gathering. Choose from:

  • Single-Day Events: Perfect for one-day events like a meetup, Pickelball, parties, dinners or book clubs
  • Multi-Day Events: Ideal for planning trips that span several days
  • Locations: Find the ideal location for trips with friends

    • Step 4: Share & Track

    After creating an event:

    • Copy and share the event link with your group
    • Track responses in the Events tab
    • See who's available and when
    • Choose the date that works best for everyone
    • Find the preferred location for your next trip together

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q: Do my friends need accounts to respond?

    A: No! They just need the event link you share with them.

    Q: Can I edit an event after creating it?

    A: Yes, you can edit event details, add/remove dates, and update group members at any time.

    Q: What's the difference between Single-Day Event and Multi-Day Events?

    A: Single-Day is for an event like a dinner, Pickelball game, meetup, book club, etc. Multi-Day Event lets you find a span of dates that works for everyone (like weekend trips).

    Q: Can I use the same group for different events?

    A: Yes! Create a group once and use it for as many events as you like.

    Q: What does "Anonymous Responses" mean?

    A: When enabled, people can't see who voted for which dates - only the anonymous responses are shown.

    Q: Can I delete people or groups?

    A: Yes, you can remove individuals from your people list or delete entire groups. This won't affect past events.
